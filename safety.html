Spills are always possible and can happen during a race or even during practices. Although injuries can happen to any rider, observing safe riding habits can ensure that injuries are kept to a minimum; rides are kept to a maximum.

Recommended Safety Gear:

  • Racing Pants
  • Gloves
  • Helmets
  • Shoes
  • Racing Jersey

Race Worthy Bikes

  • Frame and forks must not be cracked, broken or distorted.
    Check to be sure they are the correct size and in good condition.
  • Wheels must be no larger than 53.3mm
  • Tires should be properly inflated, with no splits or separations.
  • Chains and bearing must be properly greased and tensioned.
  • Brakes must operate effectively and lock the rear wheel.
  • Pedals have effective grip
  • Handlebars no wider than allowable width of 735mm
